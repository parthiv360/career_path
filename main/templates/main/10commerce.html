OPTIONS FOR COMMERCE AFTER 10th

1. Accountancy

The Accountancy subject includes measuring, processing and communication of the crucial financial data of a firm. This subject also includes analysing as well as calculating the results of the economic steps of an organization.

2. Business Studies

Technology has developed to a greater extent today. So, various new methods of carrying the business have emerged. The candidates will get familiarized about the same by studying Business Studies after 10th.

3. Economics

The candidates who study economics ar micro and macro levels is called Economics. Economics is one of the most interesting subjects, as it offers a detailed study about how the economy in national and international markets work.

Career Options After 10th in Commerce (Without Maths)

Mathematics is a complicated subject, so many candidates opt for Commerce without mathematics. Such candidates need not worry about their career options because even without Mathematics, Commerce has equal opportunities which include:

Bachelor of Commerce

Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) is a three-year course which almost all the Commerce Universities will offer. Bachelor of Business Administration

The BBA course is a 3-year undergraduate degree which offers an array of specialisation from Finance, Marketing to Accounting and Human Resource Management. This degree is internationally recognized and offers the learners with quintessential managerial and business skills.

Bachelor of Management Studies

BMS course is for those who want to explore managerial degrees in commerce after their 12th grade. This course focuses on Management studies as it offers the students with an in-depth understanding of managerial theories, concepts as well as equips them with key communication and career skills to explore higher-level professions in this field.

Bachelor of Hotel Management

Bachelor of Hotel Management is for those candidates who wish to explore one of the lucrative career options after 10th in commerce. This course offers candidates with all the necessary knowledge and skills to build a strong career in hotel management.



Career Options After 10th in Commerce (With Maths)

If the candidate chooses mathematics, they will have all the options, even those which can be taken without mathematics. These career options include:

  • BA Economics
  • BA in English Literature
  • BA Mathematics
  • Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)
  • BBM Course
  • BCom Hons

Professional Degrees as Career Options After 10th in Commerce

Apart from the degree courses, the candidate can pursue a number of professional courses as well. The candidates can prepare for these courses while in school and in college and finish the courses along with their graduation. These professional courses include:

  • CA Course
  • CS Course
  • CMA (Cost & Management Accountant)
  • CFA Course (Chartered Financial Analyst)
