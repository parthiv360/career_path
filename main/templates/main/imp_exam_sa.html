SAT is given during your class 10th, 11th and 12th for admission to colleges in the US, CANADA, and AUSTRALIA. Click to know more.

GRE FOR MASTERS

MASTERS IN USA, CANADA, EUROPE and more.

The Graduate Record Examination (GRE) is another popular examination among Indian students aspiring to study in the US or Canada. It is a fairly standardized test that measures the verbal, mathematical and general analytical skills of the candidate, though the format has undergone quite a few changes over the years. It is an admission requirement for many graduate courses, especially in engineering and the sciences, in the US.

GMAT Required for MBA

MBA IN USA, CANADA, Europe

Management aspirants need to take the Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT), which is an admissions assessment for business colleges. Average GMAT scores are in the range of 570-580. However, for top school admissions a score of at least 700 is almost mandatory. New sections have been introduced recently, such as Integrated Reasoning, which have made the test more competitive than before.
